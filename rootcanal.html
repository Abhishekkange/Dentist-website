Root Canal Treatment: Restoring Your Dental Health with Precision

Root canal treatment, also known as endodontic therapy, is a precise and effective procedure designed to save a tooth that is severely damaged or infected. This treatment targets the innermost layers of the tooth, including the pulp and nerve, to alleviate pain, prevent further decay, and preserve your natural smile.

Why Root Canal Treatment?

When a tooth's pulp becomes infected due to deep decay, cracks, or trauma, it can lead to intense pain and the risk of tooth loss. Root canal treatment is a proven solution that not only relieves pain but also prevents the need for extraction, allowing you to maintain your natural tooth structure.

Our Process:

  • Diagnosis: Our experienced dentist will first examine your tooth and may perform X-rays to identify the extent of the damage.

  • Anesthesia:Local anesthesia will be administered to ensure your comfort throughout the procedure.

  • Cleaning and Disinfection:he infected pulp and nerve will be carefully removed from the tooth's interior.

  • Filling:The cleaned space will be filled with a biocompatible material to seal the root canals and prevent re-infection.

  • Restoration:To complete the procedure, a dental crown or filling will be placed on the tooth to restore its strength and functionality.
